    Secure Payments

    How we protect your payment details

    We use industry-standard security practices to protect your payment information when you check out.

    • All transactions use TLS (HTTPS) to encrypt data in transit.
    • We never store raw card numbers on our servers.
    • Payments are processed by our trusted partners (Stripe/PayPal or other processor).
    • Fraud detection and monitoring help block suspicious activity.
